Utah

Utah is a landlocked state in the Mountain West subregion of the Western United States. It borders Colorado to its east, Wyoming to its northeast, Idaho to its north, Arizona to its south, and Nevada to its west. Utah also touches a corner of New Mexico in the southeast. It is also a very geographically diverse state. It is home to features ranging from arid deserts to pine forests in mountain valleys.
