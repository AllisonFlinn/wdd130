Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this site is to build more revenue for our existing rafting business. We want to draw in more customers from around the country, not just locally or in state.

Audience

Who are the target customers? They are adults with enough excess income to plan a traveling vacation. What do they want? They want to get away from the norm and have an energizing adventure. What questions will be answered by them accessing the website? They will know who we are, where we are located, what types of trips we offer, our prices and deals, what local lodgings are available, and customer testimonials.
